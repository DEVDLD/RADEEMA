Connection request

For your temporary connection

This type of connection is intended for supplying water or electricity to construction sites for new projects during the construction period or during an event (filming of a film, festival or other authorized events). It is only granted to companies responsible for carrying out the project and after approval by General Management.

» Documents required:
  • Common rooms :
    • Connection request or pre-printed signed by the customer with the technical characteristics of the connection (size, power, etc.)
      •                                             
    • Photocopy of the identity card (CIN) for individuals
      •                                             
    • Status of the company or commercial register and certificate of delegation of signature for legal persons
      •                                             
    • Ownership or legal authorization from the owner if the applicant is not the owner
      •                                             
    • Authorization to build
      •                                             
    • Situation map
      •                                             
    • Set of plans approved by the competent authorities
  • For a Low Voltage connection :
    • RCD warranty certificate (approved model)
  • Pour un raccordement Moyenne Tension :
    • Request made in writing with the power of the transformer to be installed
      •                                             
    • Ground plan with exact location of the substation
      •                                             
    • Set of substation equipment plans drawn up by a company approved by RADEEMA
  • For a water connection (large caliber) :
    • Technical sheet of daily water requirements (m3 / d)
  • Fees charged to the customer :
    • Participation in infrastructure
      •                                             
    • Connection or connection cost and extension costs if applicable
      •                                             
    • Pain and care on works

N.B. : For powers not exceeding 60 KVA, the temporary supply of electricity will be ensured at low voltage if our existing equipment allows it. If necessary, the supply will be at medium voltage, and in this case the MV / LV station. will be installed either by RADEEMA for rental, or by the customer himself.

For your final connection

This type of connection or connection is intended for supplying drinking water, electricity or sanitation to a construction inside a housing estate, an industrial unit, or the supply of a building.
For individual constructions or in a housing estate, the request is made at the level of the client's attachment agency on the other hand for the industrial unit and large projects in general, the request is made at the headquarters office (av. Mohamed VI - BP 520 - guéliz) or by courier delivered and deposited at the order desk of the General Management.

» Documents required:
  • Common rooms :
    • Connection request or pre-printed signed by the customer with the technical characteristics of the connection (size, power, etc.)
      •                                                 
    • Photocopy of the identity card (CIN) for individuals
      •                                                 
    • Status of the company or commercial register and certificate of delegation of signature for legal persons
      •                                                 
    • Ownership or legal authorization from the owner if the applicant is not the owner
      •                                                 
    • Authorization to build
      •                                                 
    • Situation map
      •                                                 
    • Set of plans approved by the competent authorities
  • For a Low Voltage connection :
    • RCD warranty certificate (approved model)
  • For a Medium Voltage connection :
    • Request made in writing with the power of the transformer (s) to be installed
      •                                                 
    • Ground plan with exact location of the substation
      •                                                 
    • Set of substation equipment plans drawn up by a company approved by RADEEMA
  • For a water connection (large caliber) :
    • Technical sheet of daily water requirements (m3 / d)
  • For a sanitation connection :
    • Plan of the position of the outlet of the Wastewater sewer (EU) on the facade if it is not indicated on the set of plans
  • Fees charged to the customer :
    • Participation in infrastructure
      •                                                 
    • Connection or connection cost and extension costs if applicable
      •                                                 
    • Pain and care on works

N.B. : In the event of a new connection in an equipped area, the various contributions are to be paid by the promoter according to the specifications of the project specifications. In case of modification after receipt of the equipment work, the difference is the responsibility of the customer.
